GRAPHIC IMAGE FOR THE

SANT JOAN 2018 AWARDS

Challenge

Briefing: realization of the graphic proposal for the thirty-eighth edition of the Sant Joan Awards.

Concept: exclusivity of the Catalan language.

Catalan is a language that has its own letters and spellings, which allows fast differentiation and recognition. Therefore, my proposal seeks to unite the ideas of Literature, referring to the theme of the contest, and Catalan, to honor the language used in it.

Solution

My proposal is an alphabet apparently normal that hides something that the viewer must discover. It is a tribute to the Catalan language and a claim to its use in literature.

The reason why the letters are in lowercase is that, in prose, the capital letter is only used width proper names or at the beginning of a sentence. The rest must be in lowercase. I am not presenting independent letters among them but spellings that, combined, form words and communicate.

I have also worked with an optical effect that, through color, differentiates the spellings of a common alphabet from those of the Catalan language. This gives the viewer a clue so that he knows that the proposal hides something he must discover himself. The intention is not to reveal the trick but to show that there is magic in it. The tones used do not break with the graphic of the contest sponsors and, therefore, it does not seem like an independent proposal.

I think it is important to emphasize the importance of the Catalan language and encourage its use in literature. That's why I wanted to pay tribute to him with this proposal.

CoConcept has done a great job combining the two areas most present in the contest. For us, it is important to promote the Catalan literature and this proposal is a direct claim to its use.
