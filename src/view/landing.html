I enjoy figuring out how to make things better


I'm interested in design as a way of thinking, a driver of meaningful change and a method of creating visual systems. I work to meet the high-level strategic goals of a project, but I'm also not afraid to sweat the details. Among other things, I'm especially into user research (just ask anyone who's ever met me). Here's how I work

Who?


Every project should start focusing on understanding user behaviours, needs and motivations through observation techniques, task analysis and other feedback methodologies. The better you know the users, the more appropriate the design will be for them.

What?


During the definition stage we filter the information collected during the previous phase and keep what really adds value and leads us to new interesting perspectives, delimiting the real problem nad identifying the parts that will be key to obtaining a good and innovative result.

How?


This stage aims to generate a large number of ideas. The best ones will be selected to make a first prototype and later be tested with the users.
They will give us an essential feedback to choose the final model and improve it to adapt it perfectly to their needs.
