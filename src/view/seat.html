SEAT UNDERGROUND

Challenge

Briefing: realization of a proposal on the circulation of cars in 2025. As the topic was very broad, we focused on the problem that many people have to park.

Solution

Underground parking that consists of a platform that is activated when placed on top with the car and an elevator and conveyor belts that park it and return it to you. This proposal is a new bussiness model for the multinational Seat, whic is interested in adapting its services to the mobility of the future.

I was surprised by the rigor with which the team has worked. From the Design Thinking methodology, they've come to solve a challenge that asked to meet many needs.
